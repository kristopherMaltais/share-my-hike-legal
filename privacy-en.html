Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Share My Hike handles user data. The app is designed to collect no personal information.

1. No Personal Data Collected

The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information.

2. Local Device Storage

User preferences and purchase state may be stored locally on the device.

3. External Dynamic Content

The app loads data (images, JSON) from external hosting such as GitHub Pages. No personal data is shared.

4. In-App Purchases

All payments are handled by Apple. The developer has no access to payment information.

5. No Ads or Tracking

No advertisements, analytics, or tracking technologies are used.

6. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated if the app evolves.
