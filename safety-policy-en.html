Zero Tolerance Policy regarding Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

At Share My Hike, user safety is our top priority. We enforce a strict zero-tolerance policy regarding any content or behavior related to Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE).

1. Strict Prohibitions

It is strictly prohibited to use the Share My Hike application to create, upload, share, or distribute content that:

  • Depicts, encourages, or promotes sexual abuse or exploitation of children.
  • Sexualizes minors in any way.
  • Attempts to contact minors for sexual purposes (grooming).

2. Actions Taken

  • Any content identified as violating this policy will be immediately removed.
  • The responsible user account will be permanently banned without notice.

3. Reporting to Authorities

In accordance with the law and safety standards, Share My Hike will report any confirmed cases of Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) to the appropriate authorities, including the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) or applicable local law enforcement.
